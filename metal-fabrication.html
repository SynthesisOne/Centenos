Metal Fabrication

Centeno's Commercial Heating, Ventilating & Air Conditioning Services specializes in comprehensive metal fabrication, including structural design-build, sheet metal ductwork, mechanical pipe fabrication, and custom plasma cutting solutions.

Strategically located in Houston, Texas, Centeno's operates a state-of-the-art 20,000-square-foot automated fabrication facility, fully equipped to handle custom projects tailored to specific client needs and industry standards.

Design-Build

Centeno's design-build methodology streamlines project execution by providing clients with a dedicated multi-point-of-contact service. By managing all phases of design and fabrication internally, we accelerate project timelines, significantly reduce costs, minimize design conflicts, and enhance project accuracy and reliability.

Fabrication Technology

Centeno's employs advanced modeling technologies, combining traditional construction methods with sophisticated Building Information Modeling (BIM). This approach proactively identifies and mitigates potential conflicts early in the design stage, reducing the risk of costly errors and minimizing delays that could lead to project penalties.
